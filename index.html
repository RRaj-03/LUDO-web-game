Start Menu

Instruction

  1. Choose Number of player
    Choose Number of player from Start Menu or from the Form below ludo
  2. SafePoints
    All the coloured boxes are safepoints as well as starting point for respective color
  3. Moving token
    • After generating number from the dice select the number card and then press the token you want to move.
    • If you have no tokens to move, then your chance will be skipped
  4. How to Win
    To Win just make sure to reach the endpoints with both of your tokens. endpoint is previous to the starting box
  5. Three 6 Rule
    As you know if you get a 6 on the dice you get extra throw of the dice but if get three 6 in row you loose your chance Turn is previous to the starting box
